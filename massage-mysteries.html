Breathe in, breathe out

Take a minute to...

  1. Lie down, preferably comfortably
  2. Close your eyes
  3. Breathe in slowly for up to five seconds
  4. Breathe out slowly for up to five seconds
  5. Wait one or two seconds
  6. Repeat breathing pattern four times (five total)

Just relax

Go on, stop...

  1. Sit down as you would normally
  2. Look straight ahead, and one at a time:
  3. Relax your cheeks
  4. Open your mouth slightly and relax your jaw
  5. Keep breathing!
  6. Relax your shoulders, let them sink
  7. Drop and relax your arms/hands
  8. Relax your legs and feet
  9. Breathe in and out slowly for a minute
  10. After breaths, scan your body for tension and relax again
